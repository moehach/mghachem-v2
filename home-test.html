What do I do?

I am a customer and user experience (CX/UX) researcher and designer here to help you create memorable experiences using evidence-based approaches to research and design. With me by your side, you won’t have to worry about strategic design decisions based on guesswork. I leverage evidence backed by data to create actionable plans with measurable outcomes.

I help businesses create meaningful experiences by identifying the correct strategy and approach we need to take to align customer and business needs together and bring the customer experience to life.
