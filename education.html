Education Never Ends.

I believe education is a never ending process of self-improvement that can allow you and society to create real tangible change.

Keep Learning

I make it a point to keep learning.

Education doesn't end at the university level, nor does it begin there. Real education starts when you seek subjects and chase them with passion.

My interests vary, and you'll find there's a common theme in what I study:

Society, Technology, and Design.

These topics interest me because at the intersection of these three subjects lies the ability to create something that can produce a long-lasting impact on society.
